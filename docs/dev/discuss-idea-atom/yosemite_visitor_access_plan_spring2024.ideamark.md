```yaml
entity: "IE-PILOT-RATIONALE"

  state: "confirmed"
```
Pilot builds on 2020-2022 lessons and addresses 2023 challenges without reservations.

#### IdeaEntity: IE-PILOT-RATIONALE
The 2024 pilot tests reservations without pandemic constraints or major construction.

### OCC-YOSE-05-002 : Reservation Windows
```yaml
occurrence_id: "OCC-YOSE-05-002"
entity: "IE-PILOT-WINDOWS"
role: "mechanism"
status:
  state: "confirmed"
```
Reservation requirements vary by date ranges and days of week during peak hours.

#### IdeaEntity: IE-PILOT-WINDOWS
Specific windows cover Apr 13-Jun 30 (weekends/holidays), Jul 1-Aug 16 (daily), and Aug 17-Oct 27 (weekends/holidays).

### OCC-YOSE-05-003 : Reservation Sales and Types
```yaml
occurrence_id: "OCC-YOSE-05-003"
entity: "IE-PILOT-SALES"
role: "mechanism"
status:
  state: "confirmed"
```
Reservations are sold online with daily releases and full-day/half-day options.

#### IdeaEntity: IE-PILOT-SALES
Sales are via recreation.gov with an additional daily quota released one week in advance.

### OCC-YOSE-05-004 : Reservation Exemptions
```yaml

status:
  state: "confirmed"
```

#### IdeaEntity: IE-PILOT-EXEMPTIONS
Existing in-park reservations act as access eligibility during the pilot.

# 1. What IdeaMark Is

**Part:** 1 — Philosophy  
**Status:** Draft  
**Type:** Informative / Conceptual

IdeaMark is not a document format, a knowledge base, or an ontology.

IdeaMark is an architectural foundation for making intellectual activities reusable across people, organizations, domains, and generations.

Its purpose is not to preserve meaning.

Its purpose is to preserve accessibility to meaning.

Meaning remains in authoritative original sources and is reconstructed by humans and AI under new situations.

IdeaMark therefore represents neither knowledge nor meaning directly.

It represents reusable structures that allow future intellectual activities to begin from authoritative original sources.

## Design Rationale

Knowledge systems traditionally preserved answers.

IdeaMark preserves the ability to rediscover, reinterpret, and reuse intellectual activities under future conditions.

This distinction is intentional.

If meaning were fixed inside IdeaMark, future users and AI systems would be constrained by one prior interpretation. By preserving access structures rather than interpretations, IdeaMark allows meaning to be reconstructed under new situations, literacy levels, domains, and projections.

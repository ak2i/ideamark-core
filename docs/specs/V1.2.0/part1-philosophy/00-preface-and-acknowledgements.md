# 0. Preface and Acknowledgements

**Part:** 1 — Philosophy  
**Status:** Draft  
**Type:** Informative

## Preface

Every generation inherits intellectual activities from previous generations.

The purpose of IdeaMark is not to preserve those activities exactly as they were, but to make them discoverable, reusable, and reconstructable under future situations.

In this sense, IdeaMark itself is one intellectual activity built upon countless others.

## Acknowledgements

IdeaMark is not intended as a rejection of previous research or existing information technologies.

The ideas presented in this specification emerged through continued study of knowledge engineering, information retrieval, software architecture, systems engineering, cognitive science, and artificial intelligence.

Many concepts in this specification are reinterpretations, reorganizations, and integrations of ideas developed by researchers and practitioners over many decades.

IdeaMark should therefore be understood as an attempt to extend the accumulated achievements of previous generations toward AI-enabled intellectual collaboration.

This specification represents one architectural direction rather than a definitive solution and is expected to evolve through future implementation, discussion, and research.
